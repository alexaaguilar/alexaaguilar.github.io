A design campaign created for the indie-rock band The Drums. The purpose of this design campaign was to visually capture The Drums unique style of music. The band has a distinct California beach scene sound, with some grunge undertones, which I tried to capture in this campaign. Included in the full package is the presentation deck with the inspiration moodboard, three posters, three billboards, and apparel.
